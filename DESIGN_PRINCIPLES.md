# Design Principles

## Protocols before implementations

The form of a thing must be defined before the means of carrying it are chosen.

## Observation before interpretation

A record must be preserved before it is explained.

## History before efficiency

Continuity matters more than speed.

## Stewardship before ownership

What is held in trust is not possessed.

## Propagation before hierarchy

A living infrastructure spreads through relation rather than command.

## Raw observations are permanent

The primary record remains intact.

## Knowledge evolves

Interpretation may change. The record remains.

## Protocols outlive technology

A good protocol may survive many forms of implementation.
