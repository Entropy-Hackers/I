# Manifest

This repository is the Public Constitutional Archive of I.

I is a living, constitutional, distributed, public scientific infrastructure — a protocol for building living scientific infrastructure.

A scientific infrastructure is maintained by discipline rather than completed by ceremony. The record must remain intact. The interpretation must remain distinct from the record. The inheritance of form must be kept separate from the authority to govern.

I is being defined, in language, in protocol, in stewardship, and in continuity, so that it may endure across generations of inquiry.
